About me

Hi, I am Xinhe Liu (Nickname Mindstorm233). I am currently preparing for my graduation project about “Research on Cell Interaction Analysis Based on Large-scale Pre-trained Models of Single-Cell Transcriptomics” in BUPT&THU. I am also an intern in Biomedical Engineering School of THU, where I focus on AI for Science (Biology), combining organoids technology with large foundation models.

My background includes multiple areas related to EE/CS/ML, including telecommunication, embedded development, computer vision and computational biology. 4-year Bachelor’s study journey in BUPT has given me broadened horizon and open-minded style of thinking. And that’s why I choose the interdisciplinary subject BME as my Ph.D. research direction. If you’d like to connect, please feel free to reach out.
